Beep, Back Again!

May 15, 2025 · beep

It’s alive! After a long sleep and many sidetracks, the blog is back online.

What started as a playground for dusty homelab notes and clustering experiments has finally evolved into something semi-readable. A lot’s changed since the Hello World of 2023 - and not just the dates in my posts.

What happened?#

My original Hugo blog — complete with its lone “Hello World” post - didn’t last long. Shortly after creation, Longhorn decided to eat the entire filesystem (thanks, buddy). Then I forgot to renew the domain, and it vanished into the digital void. Classic.

After that, I got very sidetracked rebuilding my cluster to better suit my needs (yes, there will be posts about that — eventually). And now? We’re back. Finally.

The blog’s still a little plain and rough around the edges, but this time, I plan to keep it breathing. Probably.

So what’s new?#

  • New Hugo build with a streamlined setup
  • LLMs in the homelab (yes, they live in the server closet now)
  • Characters like Lyra, Echo, and Zeta dropping in with blog post opinions
  • K3s cluster + Proxmox + Ceph + mild chaos
  • And of course: blog sidequests

It’s all part of the fun.

Coming soon (or eventually):

  • Nerdy tools I built by accident
  • Random AI thoughts from characters who think I work for them
